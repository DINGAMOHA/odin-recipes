Lablebi Recipe

lablebi, also known as Tunisian Chickpea Soup or Stew, is a hearty North African meal featuring chickpeas, rustic bread, warm spices, and more. Served with a variety of garnishes, my version of this cozy, comforting dish makes an easy spicy dinner.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cans of chickpeas
  • rustic bread(golden perfection)
  • extra virgin olive oil
  • yellow onions and garlic
  • spices(salt,cumin,coriander and paprika)
  • harisa paste
  • lemons

Recipe instructions:

  1. prepare the chickpeas by putting it in saucepan with there liquid cover them with water and bring the liquid to boil then lower the heat and after that let the beans simmer
  2. toast the bread by tearing the bread into pieces and give them a good drizzle of olive oil and then let them in the oven until the bread is golden brown
  3. saute the aromatics by adding more olive oil on non stick skillet over medium heat once the oil begin to shimmer add onion,garlic and spices. cook the Ingredients until the mixture is fragrant
  4. season the chickpeas by transferring the onion mixture to the chickpeas and give them a good stir and you can adjust the seasonings
  5. and finally grab your serving bowl and add the bread toast at the bottom and pour the chickpeas mixture after it and then mix and Enjoy.
