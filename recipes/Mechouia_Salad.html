Mechouia Salad Recipe

this grilled vegetable dish is often served as a side dish or as a main course with couscous, but it can also be used as an appetizer before dinner. It consists of simple and inexpensive ingredients that can be easy to find in your local grocery stores

Ingredients

  • bell pepper
  • eggplant
  • olive olive
  • garlic cloves
  • onion
  • tomatoes
  • shallot
  • chili
  • cilantro
  • lemon juice
  • salt and pepper

Recipe instructions:

  1. wash the vegetables well before you start. grill or roast all of you ingredients
  2. open flame on high heat to grill and turn until regularly turn until cooked
  3. for roasting, preheat the oven to 420°F/220°C Put your tomatoes, bell pepper, garlic cloves, and onion into a baking tray and pour over with olive oil, salt, and pepper. Take them out of the oven after 30 minutes.
  4. Chop all of your cooked vegetables into fine dice or thin slices before mixing them well. Add chili, cilantro, and lemon juice before serving on a table with any type of bread. Enjoy!
